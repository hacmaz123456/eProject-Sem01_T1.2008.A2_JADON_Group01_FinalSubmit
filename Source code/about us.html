WHO WE ARE AND OUR MISSION


As a leading event organization agency that assures to deliver our customers only the greatest quality of event services with ideal venues, essential equipment, facilities, and professional staff and technicians. In putting customer satisfaction as our utmost priority, JADON has been valued and trusted by thousands of customers, companies, and organizations. Whether it’s a birthday party, wedding, meeting conference, or any types of event, it is our pleasure to offer you and your guests a pleasant experience with the best services.


Meet Our Team Members

Steve Catling - Director

After attending Bournemouth University where he achieved a BA (Hons) degree in Television Production, Steve moved into Events Management industry as a Project Executive. Working his way up over a number of years, his background includes experience in both the agency and corporate side of events. Having worked as an Event Manager for Lloyds Banking Group, his understanding of the corporate environment and what is important when working with an agency, has been central to developing the ethos of Hippo Events. Steve places a great emphasis on a proactive approach to working. The answer is always “yes” as he values the importance of honesty and use of initiative in all aspects of work. An infectious personality, he takes great pride in establishing strong relationships with all Hippo’s clients and suppliers, adding that personal touch to every stage. Steve was recently selected as a Finalist for the industry renowned C&IT A-List award.

Jilian Schranz - Head Coordinator

Jillian has worn many hats at Event 360 in the past 11 years – from fundraising coach to project manager, to event IT support. She knows our work and our team inside and out. In her current role as Director of Business Development, she can perfectly partner our clients with the services they need and ensure our team delivers flawless event experiences that yield happy participants and stellar results. On the event, you may find her making sure our client gets the perfect “Victory Pose” photo at the finish line, or putting the finishing touches on a “Top Fundraiser” perks tent.

Tim Brockman - Team Coordinator

As one of our co-founders, Tim Brockman brings more than 20 years in the event operations industry to Event 360 and in addition to leading the team that has planned and executed some of the country’s most successful fundraising events, he has managed events for international performing artists including Phil Collins, AC/DC, Ozzy Osbourne and Genesis. Tim’s skills and experience make him uniquely qualified to provide executive level oversight to the aspects of event fundraising that are critically important, but that live behind the scenes. With his teams, Tim has personally managed the operations, finances, risk management and logistics for hundreds of large-scale events. He has a keen eye for detail and a sixth-sense for quality control. The front end and back end planning and implementation of every project we do must meet his demanding criteria.
